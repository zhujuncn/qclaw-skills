# Betting Markets Analysis Guidelines

## Market-Specific Analysis

### 1. Match Odds (1X2)

**Key Indicators:**
- Momentum > 50: Favor team with momentum
- xG differential > 0.5: Favor higher xG team
- Red card: Strong advantage to opposing team

**Avoid when:**
- Score is 0-0 at 75+ minutes
- Both teams have < 0.3 xG

### 2. Over/Under 0.5 Goals

**BACK Over when:**
- Combined xG > 1.2
- Momentum trending positive (> 30)
- > 8 total shots
- Match time > 60 minutes and 0-0

**LAY Over (or BACK Under) when:**
- Combined xG < 0.8
- < 5 total shots
- Match time > 75 minutes and 0-0

### 3. Over/Under 2.5 Goals

**BACK Over 2.5:**
- Combined xG > 2.0
- Both teams > 0.8 xG each
- > 15 total shots
- Open, end-to-end play

**BACK Under 2.5:**
- Combined xG < 1.5
- < 10 total shots at 60+ minutes
- One team defending deep

### 4. Both Teams to Score (BTTS)

**YES when:**
- Both teams xG > 0.5
- Both teams > 4 shots
- Open match with counter-attacks

**NO when:**
- One team xG < 0.2
- One team < 2 shots
- Dominant team camping in opponent half

### 5. Asian Handicap

**Use when:**
- Clear favorite with momentum
- Red card situation
- One team has > 1.0 xG advantage

### 6. Next Goal

**Favor team when:**
- Momentum > 40 in their favor
- Recent substitution (attacking player)
- Opponent defender sent off
- xG last 15 minutes > 0.4

## Timing Strategies

### Early Match (0-30 min)
- Wait for pattern to establish
- Avoid unless clear dominance

### Mid Match (30-60 min)
- Standard entry points
- React to tactical changes

### Late Match (60-90 min)
- 0-0 at 70 min: BACK Over 0.5
- Leading team at 80 min: LAY Winner
- Desperation tactics increase goal probability

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐: 3-4% of bankroll
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐: 2-3% of bankroll
- ⭐⭐⭐: 1-2% of bankroll

### Cash Out Triggers
- Goal scored: Cash out 50%
- Momentum reversal: Full cash out
- Red card: Reassess position

### Avoid
- Matches with < 0.5 combined xG at 60 min
- Heavy favorite already leading by 2+
- Weather delays or interruptions
